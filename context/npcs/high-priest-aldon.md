# High Priest Aldon

## Description
Reported to be an elf (per tavernkeep Mira, s17 — matched the Silent Coast survivor's description from s11). The party has only heard the name in fragments — once from a frightened Silent Coast survivor (s11), once scratched into the ledger found in the wrecked caravan (s16), and once confirmed by Mira when shown the cult symbol (s17).

## Voice
Unknown.

## Motivations
1. Lead the Silent Flame to whatever it is they're aiming at.
2. (Speculation) Personal investment in the Iron Reach expansion.

## Secrets
- Almost everything. The party knows the name and the reported race (elf); they don't know where Aldon is, why an elf is leading a Silent Coast cult into Iron Reach, or what the Silent Flame actually wants. Kira has not yet connected the elf detail to her brother Vael — leave that hook for the DM to spring.

## Knows about
- The cult's actual purpose.
- Probably the party's existence (Verros has reported them by now).

## History

- **s16** — Name appeared in the caravan-driver's ledger as "*Aldon's people pay double*."
- **s17** — Mira confirmed name + race when shown the cult symbol. Race: elf. Party now knows Aldon is an elf cult leader.
